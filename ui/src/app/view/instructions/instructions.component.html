Below are the explanations of the timestamps. Please fill these timestamps as detailed as possible and add every change that is communicated by the Terminal Operator, Port, or you as Carrier. This will give us a detailed view of how often the estimated times of arrival, cargo operations and departure change. Note that for cargo operations, not every change is absolutely necessary, just a good overview of the ETC 12 hours, 6 hours and 3 hours before the end. Thank you very much for your time and effort!

ETA BERTH
Estimated time of arrival at berth of vessel
RTA BERTH
Time of arrival at berth as requested by the terminal operator
PTA BERTH
Planned time of arrival at berth. When a vessel operator (or his agent) confirmed the requested time of arrival berth to the terminal operator.
ETA PBP
Estimated time of arrival at pilot boarding place or zone. This is communicated by the vessel / agent to the port authority.
RTA PBP
Time of arrival at pilot boarding place as requested by the port authority or pilot
PTA PBP
Planned time of arrival at pilot boarding place. When a vessel operator (or his agent) confirmed the requested time of arrival PBP to the pilot/port authority.
ATA PBP
The actual time of arrival of the vessel at Berth
ATA BERTH
The actual time of arrival of the vessel at pilot boarding place
ATS CARGO OPS
The actual time at which the cargo operations has started (first commercial lift)
ETC CARGO OPS
Estimated time of completion cargo operations. The estimate is usually based on the estimated productivity to perform the total number of moves
RTC CARGO OPS
The requested time at which the vessel operator or agent would like the cargo operations to finish (last commercial lift) e.g. The vessel operator might request an earlier departure in case of cut & run
PTC CARGO OPS
The agreed upon planned time of completion of cargo operations
ATC CARGO OPS
The actual time of completion of cargo operations (last commercial lift)
ETD BERTH
The estimated time of departure from the berth
RTD BERTH
The requested time of departure from berth, as requested by the port authority
PTD BERTH
Planned time of departure of the vessel from berth. This is the confirmation from the vessel of the requested time of departure done by the vessel operator/agent or captain
ATD BERTH
Actual time of departure from berth
